About TrueHire

TrueHire was born out of a simple yet powerful idea: hiring should be about skills, not biases. Our platform is the culmination of this vision, created by a passionate team of innovators during the xto10x hackathon.

We believe the traditional hiring process is broken. Resumes don't adequately showcase real skills, filters create unnecessary bias, and job posts often lack clarity. TrueHire fixes these problems with a revolutionary approach to matching talent with opportunity.

Our AI-powered platform listens, learns, and finds the perfect job-fit, matching candidates to roles with skill-first precision. No more wading through endless resumes or worrying about unconscious bias â€“ just real opportunities based on real skills.

Our Mission

To democratize the hiring process by eliminating bias and focusing on what truly matters: skills, potential, and fit. We're creating a world where opportunity is accessible to everyone with the right abilities, regardless of background.
